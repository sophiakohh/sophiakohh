Night Safari

World's First Nocturnal Wildlife Park




13-time winner of the Best Attraction category awarded by Singapore Tourism Board.
This internationally acclaimed leisure attraction embodies innovation and creativity in
products and services, and service quality, thus attracting approximately 1.3 million visitors annually.
Close to 900 animals from approximately 100 species (of which almost 41 per cent are threatened) inhabit the 35-hectare park.
In line with its mission to promote biodiversity, the park focuses on the captive breeding of threatened species.
Over the years, it has bred Malayan tigers, Asian elephants, fishing cats, red dholes, anoas,
markhors, bantengs, Malayan tapirs and Asian lions, among other threatened species.
